General Information

PROJECT

The design is focused on detecting humans, specifically a courier who works in any food delivery services platforms in the Philippines with a demand prediction system. Historical data will be applied to perform data forecasting for the following days, months, or a year.

SDG

Goal 8: Promote sustained, inclusive and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment and decent work for all.

CLIENT

KONEKSIYON (bean+bear) restaurant is located at Congressional Ave, 1106 Quezon City, Philippines which offers food and beverages services.

MISSION

To provide the business with valuable information that the business can use to make decisions about the future of the venture.

Features

The basic needs

  • Camera Detection
  • Real-time monitoring
  • Couriers detection
  • Historical Data
  • Weeks January
  • Weeks February
  • Weeks March
  • Data Forecast
  • Days Analysis
  • Weeks Analysis
  • Months Analysis
  • Year Analysis
